About Me

I am a talented, highly motivated individual with a variety of experiences seeking to excel in web development. I recently graduated from the Full-Stack Web Development Program at Southern Methodist University. Additionally, I earned my Bachelor’s of Science in Marketing at The John H. Sykes College of Business at The University of Tampa.


During my time in the full-stack web development program, I gained hands-on experience efficiently coding websites and applications using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Node, Express, React, MySQL, and MongoDB. Building state-of-the-art, user-friendly websites and applications is truly a passion of mine.


While interning for two years with TPC Tampa Bay, I enhanced my digital marketing skills using software applications such as Salesforce, MailChimp and Microsoft Office. Additionally, I was in charge of managing the company’s mobile app which led to over 900 IOS and 200 Android downloads within six months.


Throughout my career I have demonstrated effective communication skills, composure, and a professional attitude. I continue to seek out new technologies and stay up-to-date on industry trends and advancements.
